# Product Manager Track

## Goal

Show product thinking through clear problem framing, user research, prioritizing,
roadmaps, and measurable product decisions.

## Proof Signals

- Product requirement documents.
- Opportunity sizing.
- User journey maps.
- Roadmap and prioritization rationale.
- Launch and feedback plan.

## Project Ideas

- AI product spec.
- Feature teardown.
- Metrics dashboard concept.
