# Full-Stack AI Engineer Track

## Goal

Show credible end-to-end ability across product UI, AI workflows, and the
backend contracts that support them.

## Proof Signals

- Public projects showing both interface and system behavior.
- Retrieval, tool, or agent workflows described in plain language.
- Architecture notes or diagrams that explain the full flow.
- Observability or guardrail thinking, not only feature polish.
- Honest mocked-vs-real boundaries.

## Project Ideas

1. Agent workflow console with visible retrieval, tool, approval, and output states.
2. Retrieval-backed product feature with frontend citations and backend contracts.
3. AI operations panel showing status, fallback, and human override states.

## Interview Topics

- Frontend/backend contract design for AI features.
- Retrieval and tool execution flow.
- Latency and reliability tradeoffs.
- Observability and guardrail design.
- Human approval and recovery flows.
