# Resume Tailoring Guide

Use `resume-lead-frontend-final.md` for Lead Frontend, Senior Angular, Frontend Architect, React/Angular/TypeScript Lead, Enterprise UI, and Micro-frontend roles. Use `resume-ai-frontend-final.md` for AI Frontend, Copilot UI, Agent Workspace, AI tools, and enterprise automation UX roles.

## 1. Angular Jobs

### Emphasize

- Angular, TypeScript, RxJS, Angular Material, Web Components, micro-frontend architecture, enterprise SaaS, component libraries, API-driven workflows.
- Talview and Codifyd experience first.
- Team leadership, code reviews, requirement analysis, estimation, and Agile delivery.

### Reduce

- React-first wording unless the job asks for React.
- Older libraries such as jQuery unless the job mentions legacy modernization.

### Summary Variant

Lead Frontend Engineer with 18 years of hands-on web/UI development experience, including 14+ years of professional frontend engineering experience. Strong in Angular, TypeScript, Web Components, micro-frontend architecture, enterprise SaaS UI, code reviews, client communication, estimation, and Agile frontend delivery.

## 2. React Jobs

### Emphasize

- React, TypeScript, JavaScript, Node.js, Web Components, component architecture, enterprise SaaS, code reviews, and product delivery.
- Talview and Codifyd experience using React.
- Frontend leadership and architecture rather than only Angular depth.

### Reduce

- Angular-only language in the title.
- Older framework/tool lists unless needed.

### Summary Variant

Lead Frontend Engineer with 18 years of hands-on web/UI development experience, including 14+ years of professional frontend engineering experience across React, Angular, TypeScript, JavaScript, Node.js, Web Components, and enterprise SaaS platforms. Experienced in leading teams, designing scalable UI systems, and delivering complex web applications in Agile environments.

## 3. Lead Frontend Roles

### Emphasize

- Team leadership, hiring support, training, mentoring, code reviews, estimation, stakeholder communication, Agile planning, and production support.
- Talview management responsibilities and Codifyd Frontend Manager experience.
- FORTO training of 4 frontend developers.

### Reduce

- Generic hands-on coding bullets.
- Long lists of old tools.

### Summary Variant

Lead Frontend Engineer with 18 years of hands-on web/UI development experience, including 14+ years of professional frontend engineering experience. Experienced in leading frontend teams, supporting hiring and training, reviewing code, estimating work, communicating with clients, and delivering Angular/React/TypeScript applications in Agile environments.

## 4. Frontend Architect / Micro-frontend Roles

### Emphasize

- Web Components, Micro-Frontend Architecture, scalable UI systems, component libraries, enterprise SaaS, Angular/React architecture, API-driven workflows.
- Talview and Codifyd bullets about modular frontend delivery and technical decisions.
- Verdantis work on 20+ modules and custom widgets.

### Reduce

- Simple feature-delivery phrasing.
- Unrelated backend/database details unless the role is full-stack.

### Summary Variant

Frontend Architect and Lead Frontend Engineer with 18 years of hands-on web/UI development experience, including 14+ years of professional frontend engineering experience. Experienced in Angular/React architecture, Web Components, micro-frontend patterns, component libraries, scalable UI systems, and enterprise SaaS delivery.

## 5. AI Frontend / Copilot UI Roles

### Emphasize

- AI Employee Force / Angular AI Copilot Platform.
- Copilot UI, agent marketplace, Codex-style session UI, collapsible sidebar, agent workspace, browser/testing panel, tool execution UX, LLM streaming interface, enterprise automation UX.
- Angular, TypeScript, Node/NestJS-style architecture boundaries.

### Avoid

- Do not claim ML engineering, data science, model training, MLOps, or AI research.
- Do not make the AI project sound like a production employer project unless it is independently deployed and documented.

### Summary Variant

AI Frontend Engineer and Lead Frontend Engineer with 18 years of hands-on web/UI development experience, including 14+ years of professional frontend engineering experience. Experienced in Angular, React, TypeScript, Web Components, micro-frontend architecture, and enterprise SaaS delivery. Currently building AI-powered frontend systems including copilot-style workspaces, agent marketplaces, LLM streaming interfaces, and enterprise automation UX.

## 6. International Roles

- Mumbai, India | Open to relocation / remote.
- Germany exposure through FORTO in Berlin.
- Client communication, Agile delivery, production support, and remote-ready collaboration.

Lead Frontend Engineer with 18 years of hands-on web/UI development experience, including 14+ years of professional frontend engineering experience. Experienced in remote-ready frontend delivery through clear requirement analysis, estimation, code reviews, client communication, Agile execution, and production support.

## Keywords to Remove or Reduce

- Full-stack Programmer, unless the role is explicitly full-stack.
- Generic “HTML/CSS/JavaScript coding” bullets.
- Facebook, Twitter, hobbies, school details, full address, and “Thank you.”
- Old tools that are not relevant to the job, unless the role mentions legacy modernization.

## Metrics to Add When Confirmed

- Number of frontend engineers managed at Talview and Codifyd.
- Number of modules, applications, or product areas owned.
- Number of clients or stakeholders supported.
- Performance improvements beyond the confirmed 46% at FORTO.
- Defect reduction, release cadence, sprint delivery improvement, or code review throughput.
- Scale of the Verdantis platform: users, transactions, modules, languages, or regions.

## Tailoring Workflow

1. Match the title to the job: Lead Frontend Engineer, Senior Angular Developer, Frontend Architect, AI Frontend Engineer, or Micro-frontend Architect.
2. Rewrite the first two summary lines to mirror the job’s top requirements.
3. Reorder skills so the job’s required technologies appear first.
4. Move the most relevant project or achievement into the top half of the resume.
5. Remove any skill or bullet that distracts from the job target.
6. Keep claims factual and interview-defensible.
